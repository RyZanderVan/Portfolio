Who Am I?

My name is Ryan VanAlstine and I am currently studying Web Design at Saint Paul College in Saint Paul, Minnesota. Right now I am on track to hit my estimated graduation date of May 2024 where I will have earned a Web Design Certification. I made this website as a portfolio of my skills developing websites using both HTML and CSS coding.
I have taken the following classes at Saint Paul College:

  • Web Fundamentals
  • 2D Web Animation
  • Adobe Animate
Next semester I will be taking Web Design, Client Side Programming, and Graphical Web Design which will fulfil the requirements of my certification.

My hobbies include; reading usually horror or fantasy novels, constantly getting my heart broken by Minnesota Sports, and all things video games whether it be playing them and writing or reading about them.

2024 marked the 2nd year in a row that we decided to head up to Duluth, MN over the Thanksgiving holiday and just like last year it was a fantastic idea! We celebrated by bundling up and exploring around Canal Park, shopping, watching the boats on the water, and taking a stroll through the famout Bentlyville Christmas Lights Display.
